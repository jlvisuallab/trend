About Us

TREND ELECTRONICS (M) Sdn. Bhd. is a trading company for semiconductor and PCBA products and solutions located in Penang, Malaysia.
We established longer and closer working relationships with our clients and suppliers, not only provide immediate and reliable service but also to continually exceed expectations and goals.

We offer a wide range of high performance products in various forms, including wave & selective soldering, reflow oven, test & inspections machine, laser marking machine, soldering pallets, jig tools and etc.

We look forward to welcoming you to our company and work to develop and enhance your current business.
